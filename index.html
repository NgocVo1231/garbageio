Garbage Items Bins Facts
Packing Peanuts Trash Unfortunately, packing peanuts are not able to be recycled or composted. However, they can be safely disposed of in the trash.
Newspapers Recycle Newspapers are made of paper, which can be used to make other items once processed in a recycling plant.
Used Syringe Hazardous Used syringes can pose a health risk to others if put in the trash. Please dispose of them at a facility that processes biohazards.
Old Batteries E-Waste Some old batteries contain mercury and other dangerous metals, which harm the environment. Please dispose of them at a facility that processes e-waste.
Used Coffee Filters Compost Used coffee filters and even tea bags are biodegradable, so they can be composted. After composted items decompose they can be used as fertilizer.
Empty Soda Can Recycle Cans are made of aluminum, which can be used to make other items once processed in a recycling plant. If you’re not sure if something is recyclable, check for a recycling logo on it.
Glass Bottle Recycle Glass is a recyclable material, which can be used to make other items once processed in a recycling plant. If you’re not sure if something is recyclable, check for a recycling logo on it.
LED Bulb E-waste Some fluorescent bulbs contain mercury, which harms the environment. Please dispose of them at a facility that processes e-waste.
Old Medication Hazardous Old medication can pose a health risk to others if put in the trash. Please dispose of them at a facility that processes biohazards.
Banana Peel Compost Banana peels and other food waste are biodegradable, so they can be composted. After composted items decompose they can be used as fertilizer.
